# Getting Started

Get started on Shape Network.

## 1. Add Shape to Your Wallet

1. Go to the [Shape website](https://shape.network)
2. Click on the `Add Shape Mainnet` button. This will trigger MetaMask (or your wallet provider of choice)
3. You'll be prompted to switch to the Shape network
   - If the network isn't listed, you can add it manually with these details

## 2. Bridge to Shape

1. Choose a bridge to Shape
2. Bridge ETH over to Shape from another chain
3. Once complete, check your ETH balance on Shape
   - If you don't see it, try adding the token manually using the contract address: `0x4200000000000000000000000000000000000006`

## 3. Mint Your Stack

Mint your [Stack](https://stack.shape.network/)
